My Favorite Book:"The Great Gatsby"

"ThGreat Gatsby" is a novel writter by F.Scott Fitzgerald and was first published in 1945. It is widely considered one of the greatest American novels. The story is set during the Roaring Twenties and explores themes of love , wealth, and the American Dream.

The novel's main character, Jay Gatsby, is famous for his extravagant parties and unrequited love for Daisy Buchanan. His story ultimately leads to tragic consequences. The book is known for its symbolism and its critical view of the American Dream.
